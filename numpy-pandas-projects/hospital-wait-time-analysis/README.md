# Hospital Wait Time Analysis

This is what was gathered from the tracking of patient wait times in Ernest International Hospital. There was extensive coverage across departments.

## Analysis Insights

1. Psychiatry has the longest average total wait — 92 minutes
2. The middle 50% of patients across all departments wait between roughly 60-110 minutes, consistent across departments suggesting systemic rather than department-specific delays
3. Wait times follow a roughly normal distribution peaking at 80-120 minutes — few patients wait under 30 or over 140 minutes

## Tools Used

    - Python, pandas, numpy, matplotlib
